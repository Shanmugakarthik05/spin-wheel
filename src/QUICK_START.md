# UXcellence - Quick Start Guide

## For Participants üéØ

### Step 1: Create Your Team
1. Open the application
2. You'll see a login page with two tabs
3. Click on **"Create Team"** tab
4. Enter your desired team name (e.g., "Team Phoenix")
5. Click **"Create Team"** button
6. You'll see: ‚úÖ Team "Your Team Name" created successfully!

### Step 2: Login
1. Switch to the **"Login"** tab
2. Enter the exact team name you just created
3. Click **"Login"** button
4. You're now in your team dashboard!

### Step 3: Spin the Wheel
1. You'll see your current round information
2. Click the **"Spin Wheel"** button
3. Watch the animated wheel spin üé°
4. You'll be assigned a unique question
5. View your question details and description

### Step 4: Wait for Round Completion
1. After spinning, wait for other teams to spin
2. When all teams are done, a countdown appears: 3... 2... 1...
3. Admin will review and select teams for next round
4. If selected, you'll advance to the next round
5. If not selected, your participation ends

### Step 5: Check Your Marks (Optional)
1. After the round, admin may assign marks
2. View your score and feedback in your dashboard
3. Marks help admin decide round progression

## For Admins üë®‚Äçüíº

### Step 1: Login as Admin
1. Open the application
2. Click on **"Login"** tab
3. Enter: **UXcellence** (case-insensitive)
4. Click **"Login"**
5. You're now in the admin dashboard!

### Step 2: Monitor Team Creation
1. Teams will appear automatically as participants register
2. Go to **"Teams"** tab to see all teams
3. Teams show up in real-time (3-second sync)
4. You can also manually add teams if needed

### Step 3: Add Questions
1. Go to **"Questions"** tab
2. Click **"Add Question"** button
3. Enter the question text
4. Enter detailed description
5. Click **"Add Question"**
6. Repeat for all questions needed for the round

### Step 4: Let Teams Spin
1. Monitor teams as they spin the wheel
2. Watch the **"Teams with Questions"** counter
3. Each team gets a unique question (no duplicates)
4. Wait until all teams have spun

### Step 5: Mark/Score Teams (Optional but Recommended)
1. Go to **"Marks"** tab
2. For each team, click **"Add/Update Marks"**
3. Enter marks (0-100)
4. Add reason/comments for feedback
5. Click **"Save Marks"**

### Step 6: Select Teams for Next Round
1. Go to **"Teams"** tab
2. You'll see checkboxes next to each team
3. Check the boxes for teams that should advance
4. Or use **"Select All"** to select everyone
5. Selected count shows at the top

### Step 7: Complete Round & Advance
1. Go to **"Round Control"** tab
2. Review selected teams (shown as badges)
3. Click **"Complete Round & Advance Selected Teams"**
4. A dialog appears with options:
   - **Try Again**: Reset round, keep same teams
   - **Go to Next Round**: Advance selected teams, delete others
5. Read the warning about team deletion
6. Click **"Go to Next Round"** to confirm
7. Selected teams move to next round
8. Unselected teams are permanently deleted

### Step 8: Repeat for Rounds 2 & 3
1. Add new questions for Round 2
2. Monitor team spins
3. Mark teams
4. Select teams for Round 3
5. Complete Round 2
6. Round 3 is the final round (no advancement)
7. Declare winners based on marks!

## Common Workflows

### Scenario: Running a Competition from Start to Finish

**Before Event:**
1. Admin logs in
2. Admin adds questions for Round 1
3. Admin configures round settings (optional)

**During Event - Round 1:**
1. Participants create teams and login
2. Admin monitors team creation
3. Participants spin wheel
4. Admin marks teams
5. Admin selects top performers
6. Admin advances to Round 2

**During Event - Round 2:**
1. Admin adds questions for Round 2
2. Selected teams login and spin
3. Admin marks teams
4. Admin selects finalists
5. Admin advances to Round 3

**During Event - Round 3 (Final):**
1. Admin adds questions for Round 3
2. Finalist teams login and spin
3. Admin marks teams
4. Admin declares winners

**After Event:**
1. Admin can view all marks/scores
2. Admin can export results (future feature)
3. Admin can reset for next event

## Troubleshooting

### "Team name already exists"
**Problem**: Someone already registered with that name  
**Solution**: Choose a different team name

### "Cannot see my team in admin dashboard"
**Problem**: Sync delay  
**Solution**: Wait 3-5 seconds, it will appear automatically

### "Can't advance round"
**Problem**: Not all teams have questions OR no teams selected  
**Solution**: 
- Ensure all teams have spun the wheel
- Check at least one team in Teams tab

### "Wheel won't spin"
**Problem**: Already spun in this round  
**Solution**: You can only spin once per round, wait for admin to advance

### "Team was deleted"
**Problem**: Admin didn't select your team for next round  
**Solution**: Only selected teams advance, contact event organizer

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Participants
1. **Register early** to get your preferred team name
2. **Write down** your exact team name for login
3. **Read questions carefully** after spinning
4. **Check for marks/feedback** after each round
5. **Stay engaged** even if not advancing (learn from feedback)

### For Admins
1. **Prepare questions in advance** before participants arrive
2. **Communicate selection criteria** to participants beforehand
3. **Use marking system** for transparent evaluation
4. **Double-check selections** before advancing rounds
5. **Use "Try Again"** if you need to redo a round
6. **Keep records** of marks for final results
7. **Announce winners** based on Round 3 marks

### For Event Organizers
1. **Test the system** before the actual event
2. **Have backup questions** ready
3. **Explain rules clearly** to participants
4. **Set time limits** for each round (manual timer)
5. **Have judges ready** for marking
6. **Prepare certificates/prizes** for winners
